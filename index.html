Crazy Cat Lady

  • High Concept: The player is tasked with trying to eliminate all of the randomly spawned targets as quickly as possible.
  • Player Experience Goals: Enjoy the ridiculous concept of the game and compete to clear the game in the fastest time.
  • Genre: A parabolic projectile target shooter.
  • Game play: As a crazy cat lady, your actions consist of running around throwing cats at people. The left and right arrow keys will move the crazy cat lady, the spacebar will perform a jump, and the up arrow will throw cats. The trajectory of your cat will be relative to your own velocity, so experiment with jumping to fine-tune your throws.
  • Prototype goal: I wanted to experiment a little bit with side-scrolling and using a tilemap editor for level creation, and I was also interested to see how a gravity-based projectile could work from a gameplay standpoint. I intially was thinking of Super Ghouls and Ghosts before I started the prototype, but as I got to working on the game, I thought it would be interesting to take the danger out of it and be able to let loose as the crazy cat lady and throw cats with reckless abandon.
  • Features: Meowing ammunition
  • Setting/Premise: You are a crazy cat lady who, in attempt make the world a better place, runs around throwing cats at people.
  • Target Audience: People with a taste for the absurd and speed-runners.
  • Play time: About 30 seconds for a single play-through
